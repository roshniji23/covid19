About JAGRAN LAKECITY University

Jagran Lakecity University Bhopal is a private university established under Section 2(f) of UGC Act 1956 and is based out of Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. JLU Bhopal is one of the fastest growing and one of the most awarded university of Central India having practice based pedagogy at its core. Currently the university is offering 56 degree program to more than 2500 students from 8 countries and 27 states of India. The university has 27 partnership with top industries and international universities ensuring the students get great exposure both nationally and internationally.
