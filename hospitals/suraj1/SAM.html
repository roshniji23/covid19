About SAM GLOBAL University

SAM College of Education is one of the institutions of SAM Group of institutions. This group has been founded by Shri I.S. Saluja who had been the chairman of Bhopal Development Authority. The secretary of this society Shri Harpreet Saluja is a MBA Senior. The treasure of the society who is also the Director of the College, Mrs. Preeti Saluja is technically qualified in the field of computer education. The society is backed up by a group of professionals already pioneer in the field of education from last 30 years. The institute has been established with the main object of providing high quality technical and non-technical education to all student in a keen intellectual environment. The institute has been planned for development, with all modern facilities of Building, Laboratories, Workshops, Audio Visual Teaching aids and related infrastructure like library, computer centre, sports facilities and other recreational facilities. Well qualified and highly experienced faculty is an added asset. Apart from achieving academic excellence, the institute aims at building sound personality of students through unique teaching methodologies. The institution is managed by Shri Guru Hargovind Society, 13 Lala Lajpat Rai Colony, Raisen Road, Bhopal - 462023.
